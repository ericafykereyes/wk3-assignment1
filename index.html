Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my
top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine

  1. Sunny Side Kitchen
  2. Mariana Lighthouse
  3. Coffee Stop
  4. Surf's Club
  5. Zoom Cafe

Sunny Side Kitchen | Garapan, Saipan

Sunny Side Kitchen is the perfect place to share the table with loved ones. With the cozy interior inspired
by the simplicity of a home, this cute restaurant and bakery is a haven for hearty meals, pastries, and comfort.

What I love:

  • Freshly Baked Goods
  • Cozy atmosphere
  • The staff's great hospitality
